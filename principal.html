Message from the Principal

"Peace, Dignity and Equality on a healthy Planet" - United Nations

Dear Delegate and Faculty Advisors,

The world today stands at the intersection of a multitude of complex challenges that humankind has never seen or experienced. In the same defining moment, it also offers immense possibilities for change, innovation and impact. No machine learning or algorithms can lead change. People are the agency for transformation.

At Shiv Nadar School we believe in building in our young people, leadership attributes that instil in them values of service, tenacity, vision and foresight. To create opportunities for them to inherit the core principles enshrined in the UN Charter: of Peace, Justice, Respect, Human Rights, Tolerance and Solidarity. To build their skills and capacity; to be thought leaders of empathy, intelligence and integrity.

It is my privilege to write to you to share about the Shiv Nadar School Model United Nations Conference, to be held on 5th and 6th of August 2022. After two years of disruption and paradigm shifts in Education, we have welcomed students in our physical campuses to revive, reimagine and reinvent, experiences that leave a lasting impact. The purpose of SNSMUN is to promote the spirit of international cooperation. We have always valued the way MUNs have provided the required platform to young minds to ideate, deliberate and decide to resolve issues of global and national concern. The students gain first-hand experience on the art of negotiation and skill of diplomatic persuasion by playing the role of diplomats representing a nation. Our conference hopes to contribute towards better understanding of international matters and realities among the young leaders of tomorrow. The Conference diligently follows the UN4MUN Rules of Procedure and is an authentic simulation of the corresponding committees of the United Nations aimed at promoting collaboration and diplomacy. We look forward to your participation.

With warm regards,
Anju Wal
Director Principal
Shiv Nadar School Faridabad
