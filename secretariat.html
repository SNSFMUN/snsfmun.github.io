Message from the Secretariat

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors,

Every success story chronicles an arduous journey punctuated with tireless effort and dauntless courage. Shiv Nadar School Faridabad is dedicated to facilitating students to discover their latent talents, augmenting the unveiling of their potentials in diverse disciplines and inculcating values to help them stand tall as responsible citizens of the world. Our vision is to further push the boundaries of learning and create stimulating opportunities for our enthused learners to engage in riveting debates, discussions and become aware of their global surroundings.

The Shiv Nadar School Model United Nations Conference, to be held on August 5 – 6, 2022, promises to take this unbridled enthusiasm a step further and provide a platform to the students from different nooks and corners of the world of learning to immerse in an enriching and exciting simulation of the United Nations. We are set to dazzle the minds of the students by running committees exploring a wide array of geographical areas and time periods, enabling them to run replications of state governments, conduct peace negotiations and become a part of unconventional decision-making bodies. We endeavour to kindle their interest in the global political, social and economic issues by letting them slip into the shoes of the world leaders, allowing them the space to ruminate, discuss and expound on the subjects that matter the most. This year the committees have been chosen keeping in mind the prevalent global issues such as the omnipresent sustainable energy crisis, women’s economic empowerment, access to primary education, The Paris Agreement, and the resultant climate refugee crisis. We shall also deliberate upon the challenges of the post-pandemic era such as the looming threat of zoonotic diseases and outbreaks. We look forward to hearing how our delegates propose to tackle these situations and giving them an opportunity to understand the working of the United Nations and drafting viable resolutions. The essence of the Conference is Collaboration and Diplomacy. All participants will get participation certificates and outstanding participation will be honoured appropriately.

It is our distinct pleasure to cordially invite you to the Shiv Nadar School Model United Nations Conference to be held on August 5 – 6, 2022 at Shiv Nadar School Faridabad campus. We request your kind cooperation and whole-hearted participation to catapult our humble endeavour to the pinnacles of success. Looking forward to welcoming your delegation.

Regards,
The Secretariat
