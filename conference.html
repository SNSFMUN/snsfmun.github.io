Message from the Principal

"The UN wasn't created to make mankind into paradise, but rather, to save humanity from hell."
- Dag Hammerskjold, second Secretary-General of the United Nations

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors, 

We are living in times of tumultuous change and massive environmental and political upheaval. But as Antonio Gutiérrez, the 
UN Secretary General rightly said in his New Year’s message, "Moments of great difficulty are also moments of great 
opportunity. To come together in solidarity. To unite behind solutions that can benefit all people…." Model United Nations 
is one such platform which brings young minds together to discuss, deliberate and spur global and local change. It is the 
need of the hour to foster strong and capable leaders, thinkers and humanitarians. At SNSMUN we aim at reaffirming the 
pillars on which the United Nations was built - peace, development, and equal opportunity. With that in view, we will 
simulate 6 committees that will cover a wide array of geographical areas and socio-economic crises, and we are 
confident that every participant will be able to find the opportunity to engage and emerge as a confident diplomat.

It gives me immense pleasure to invite your esteemed institution to participate in the Shiv Nadar School Model United 
Nations Conference 2022, hosted by Shiv Nadar School Faridabad, from August 5 to 6, 2022. The purpose of SNSMUN is to 
promulgate the spirit of international cooperation. We have always valued the way MUNs have provided the required platform 
to young minds to discuss, deliberate and dream to resolve subjects of global and national concern. The students gain 
first-hand experience on the art of negotiation and skill of diplomatic persuasion by playing the role of diplomats 
representing a nation. Our conference hopes to contribute towards better understanding of international issues and 
concerns among children. We look forward to your participation. 


Some salient features of the SNSMUN are:

• The Conference scrupulously follows the UN4MUN Rules of Procedure and is a completely authentic simulation of the 
  corresponding committees of the United Nations.
• The discourse at the Conference promotes Collaboration and Diplomacy.

With warm regards,
Anju Wal
Principal
