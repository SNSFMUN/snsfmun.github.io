General Assembly Second Committee

Agenda - Ensuring Access to Affordable, Reliable, Sustainable and Modern Energy for All.

SDG 7 with the goal to ”ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all” is a priority for General Assembly Second Committee. Energy is central to nearly every major challenge and opportunity the world faces today,therefore its no surprise that affordable energy is the heart of around two third of the SDGs targets. Access to food, clean water, sanitation, education, technology, and healthcare is reinforced by affordable and clean energy. Because of the growing number of world population and pressure of urbanization, there is an expectation that the energy demand worldwide is going to increase almost 40% by 2040. However, the sector is greatly dependent on established petroleum resources.However, the challenge is far from being solved and there needs to be more access to clean fuel and technology and more progress needs to be made regarding integrating renewable energy into end-use applications in buildings, transport and industry. Public and private investments in energy needs to be optimized and there needs to be more focus on regulatory frameworks and innovative business models to transform the world’s energy systems.
